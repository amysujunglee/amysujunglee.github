Why I started a blog

January 13, 2019 by Amy Lee

I decided to start writing a blog because I want to track my career path and how I improve for a year. Blogging is one of my New Year's Resolutions for 2019.

It's my very first time and I'm definitely sure I'm very suck at writing. Please understand there will be typos and grammer errors because Ensligh is my second language. But, I will try my best to keep writing in English.

Now I'm working at Best Buy Canada as a Digital Experience Designer, and I'm trying to change my career path from a designer to a front-end developer.

I used to work as a web developer from 2014 to 2015. Officially I have 1 year of work experience in front-end development. I'm still sitting at a junior level.

Recently, I've realized that I'm very passionate on coding and building something on web-based devices.

I'm still not sure whether it's a good decision for my life or not. I just want to try and try, and I know it will be very hard to master development skills. But, what I know about at least is that I really love to code more than.. anything.
