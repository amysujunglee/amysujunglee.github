Samsung Securities UX consulting &
Online Channel Development

Overview

Samsung Securities has been a leading securities company in South Korea for many years. The company offers 3 online trading channels: MTS, Website, and HTS.
We designed To-Be customer journey and identified customer needs based on customer/competitor/Fintech trend analysis, internal and external customer survey analysis and accumulated customer data. Quick Prototyping visualized the channel to be changed. Based on the results, we completed the steps based on the UX/UI innovation roadmap and performed the following tasks.

My Roles

  • Researched the latest fintech technology, UX/UI trends and consumer behavior
  • Benchmarked the competitors, from big global companies and startups (Charles Schwab, Merrill Edge, Fidelity, Vanguard, Betterment, Wealthfront, etc)
  • Analyzed every touchpoint of customer experience journey and As-Is Heuristic evaluation analysis
  • Conducted FGDs in 4 different groups based on the customer segmentation
  • Mainly created UX/UI design for mainpage, signup page, product detail page, etc by following the UX principles and UI guidelines
  • Conducted usability testing

Work Process

(1) UX Consulting:

  • Analyzing the internal documents; Business strategy, goals, target segmentations, etc
  • Researched on the latest Fintech technologies and benchmarked currently big global companies (Merrill EDGE, Charles Schwab, Fidelity, Betterment, etc) and startups
  • Interviewed the stakeholders and conducted a FGD (Focus Group Discussion)
  • Confirmed the major steps of Customer journey, analyzed MOT (Moment Of Truth), and discovered how to improve the weakpoints
  • Throughout all the analysis results above, created Samsung Securities' UX principle and guidelines
  • Created UX/UI prototypes of online channels

(2) WEB Channel Development:

  • Created WBS (Work Breakdown Structure) and communicate/share with the client (Samsung Securities), the partner (PwC) and the team members based on it
  • Re-created IA of Samsung Securities web channel
  • Created UI guidelines and constructed each page
  • Conducted Usability test

Result

  • Based on our UX goals, we established the Samsung Securities UX concept and the three major UX design principles.
  • Also, we have created a UI principle that enables Samsung Securities’ services to bring out differentiated identities.
