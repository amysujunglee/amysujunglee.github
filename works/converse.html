'Converse' UCC Project

View Video

This video was for the contest of Converse UCC(User Created Contents). At university, I took many courses which were about UCC and made various types of UCC. For making UCC, I learned about the skills of storytelling, filming and editing. I was mostly in charge of editing.

Date: 2008
- Target users : People who usually wear Converse shoes
- Concept : Making socks like Converse shoes
- Story : Everyday people who like Converse wear Converse shoes like socks, and they want to get their unique styles by painting on their shoes. Thus, it shows the process of making one's own Converse shoes on socks.

Skills: Adobe Premiere, After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator
Involvement: 50%
