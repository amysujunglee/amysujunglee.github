Overview

At Corporate Champions Vancouver(CCV), I lead design for both web and print, mainly focusing on 2013 CCV Summer Games event bag, event banners and graphic designs. I Determined the company’s needs, collaborated with team, created the event bag and banners. Every year they hold the summer event and there are 50 companies and 3,000 participants.

My Roles

  • Assessed the company’s needs, collaborated with the team, created and re-designedthe event bags & banners
  • Created a template for batch processing of the event photos in Photoshop
  • Volunteered at the event and created the videos for the medal ceremony
  • Analyzed the company website and suggested recommendations for improvements
